About Page

Welcome to our website!

This a website made for the Information Organisation course. The domain of this website is wine and wine flavors.

Users can use this website to filter wines out of our dataset that fit their specific taste, based on wine flavors.

Let's say that an user likes to drink wines that have a black fruity flavor, and they like their wine aged. They will be able to find a wine suitable for them using the 'Black Fruit' and 'General Aging' filters.

When clicking on a wine, they will be able to see more detailed information about the wine. A description based on reviews, price, variety and points are included.
